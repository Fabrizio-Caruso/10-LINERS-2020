TREASURE CHAMBER

This is an original game by Fabrizio Caruso

This is an action game.

You have reached the treasure chamber where jewels are spawned at random position.
Collect as many jewels as you can, while escaping the 7 ghosts.
The enemies follow you by a strategy of zone marking. Each skull has a pre-assigned zone.

You start with an energy level of 8.
When your energy reaches 0, the game is over.s


CONTROLS
Use JOYSTICK in PORT 2 to move
