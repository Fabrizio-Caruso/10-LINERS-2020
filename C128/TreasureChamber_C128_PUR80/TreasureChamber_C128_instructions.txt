TREASURE CHAMBER
for the Commodore 128 (native 128 mode) in plain BASIC V7

This is an original game by Fabrizio Caruso

This is an action game.

You have reached the treasure chamber where jewels are spawned at random positions.
Collect as many jewels as you can and avoid the 7 skulls.
They follow you with a specific zone marking strategy, in that each skull has a pre-assigned zone.

You start with an energy level of 8.
When your energy reaches 0, the game is over.


CONTROLS
Use JOYSTICK in PORT 2 to move
